You continue for quite some time, feeling as if you are being lured into a trap.
Eventually, you arrive in a large clearing. It is dark by now.
In the middle of the clearing is a stone house with a light in the window.
It reminds you of a place long forgotten...
You look for your guide but they are nowhere to be found.
You decide to head towards the house, tenatively...
The door creaks open, it appears to have been kicked in long ago.
The house is a single room, with smashed up furniture, lying in the centre of the room is a pile of gold, a potion and a weapon. 
You hastily gather them up. Upon leaving the house, the light extinguishes.
In the darkness you hear a loud splintering sound. You fall...
