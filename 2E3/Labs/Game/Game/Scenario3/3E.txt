A staircase reveals itself.
You are led downwards, spiralling downwards.
Soon, you see a warm glow at the bottom of the stairwell.
The room you enter is filled with gold and jewels.
At the far end is a door.
Something deep down inside you sparks to life. You remember who you are, why you are here...
You begin to run towards the door, knowing that the end to your long and arduous quest is on the other side...
